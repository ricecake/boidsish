# Plant Generator Grammar ##############################

1 ROOT SEED

1 SEED SPIRAL_VINE
1 SEED FRACTAL_BUSH

# --- Spiral Vine ---
# Continuously translates and rotates.
# C (ControlPoint) smooths the spline without over-generating tube geometry.
0.85 SPIRAL_VINE F \ C SPIRAL_VINE
0.10 SPIRAL_VINE F \ [ & L ] SPIRAL_VINE
0.05 SPIRAL_VINE F P

# --- Fractal Bush ---
# Classic stochastic branching using stack pushes.
# H (Hub) is placed at splitting junctions.
0.75 FRACTAL_BUSH F H [ + FRACTAL_BUSH ] [ - FRACTAL_BUSH ]
0.25 FRACTAL_BUSH F L
